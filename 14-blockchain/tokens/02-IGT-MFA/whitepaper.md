## Executive Summary

The **IGT-MFA (Ministry of Foreign Affairs Token)** represents the diplomatic arm of the Sovereign Government of Ierahkwa Ne Kanienke. This token enables secure international relations, treaty management, and diplomatic communications.

---

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose
The IGT-MFA token digitizes diplomatic operations, enabling secure and transparent international relations for the sovereign nation of Ierahkwa Ne Kanienke.

### 1.2 Vision
To establish Ierahkwa Ne Kanienke as a recognized sovereign entity in the global community through blockchain-verified diplomatic credentials and agreements.
