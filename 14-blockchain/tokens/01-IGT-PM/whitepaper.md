# IGT-PM: Office of the Prime Minister Token

## Whitepaper v1.0

### Sovereign Government of Ierahkwa Ne Kanienke

---

## Executive Summary

The **IGT-PM (Office of the Prime Minister Token)** is the primary executive government token of the Sovereign Government of Ierahkwa Ne Kanienke. This token represents the highest level of executive authority and serves as the cornerstone of the government's digital infrastructure.

---

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose
The IGT-PM token digitizes and secures the executive functions of the Office of the Prime Minister, enabling transparent governance, secure transactions, and efficient administration across the sovereign nation.

### 1.2 Vision
To establish a sovereign digital economy where government operations are transparent, efficient, and accessible to all citizens of Ierahkwa Ne Kanienke.

## 5. Governance Structure

### 5.1 Token Authority
The IGT-PM token operates under the direct authority of the Office of the Prime Minister, with oversight from the Supreme Court of Ierahkwa (IGT-SC) and the Office of the Comptroller General (IGT-OCG).

### 5.2 Interoperability
This token interacts seamlessly with all 40 government department tokens, enabling coordinated governance across:
- Executive & Core (10 departments)
- Social Services (10 departments)
- Resources & Development (10 departments)
- Security & Administration (10 departments)
