## Value Mechanisms
1. **Transaction fees**: 2-12% in WMP on all platform activity
2. **Forex demand**: WMP↔USD/BRL/MXN/ARS/COP/PEN/CLP/CAD conversions
3. **Staking**: Proof of Sovereignty validators earn WMP
4. **Burn**: 0.1% of each transaction burned (deflationary)
5. **Utility**: Required for premium services, governance voting weight
6. **Remittances**: Cross-border P2P transfers across the Americas (0% fee)
7. **NFC Offline**: Transactions without internet in rural communities

## Price Stability
- BDET Bank maintains WMP/USD peg band ($0.0001-$0.001)
- Reserve fund: 10% supply (1T WMP) for market-making
- Algorithmic stabilization via AI Fortress
- No speculative trading incentives by design
- Backed by real economic activity across 35+ countries

## Anti-Speculation Measures
- Maximum wallet holding: 0.1% of supply (10B Ⓦ)
- Transfer cooldown: 1B+ Ⓦ requires 24h delay
- No listing on speculative exchanges without Council approval
- Priority: utility over speculation
