# Wampum (Ⓦ) — Token Economics Whitepaper v2.0

## Overview
Wampum (WMP) is the sovereign digital currency of the Indigenous Americas, issued and governed by BDET Bank under the Consejo Digital Soberano. Designed to serve **670+ million people** across North America, Central America, South America, and the Caribbean — all descendants of the original peoples of this continent. No borders, one currency, one people.

## Why 10 Trillion Supply
- **670M+ people** across all the Americas need sufficient liquidity
- Every person born in the Americas carries indigenous heritage — mestizo, criollo, native
- Designed for daily transactions at scale (micro-payments, P2P, NFC offline)
- Other regional tokens operate at 10T+ supply for similar population coverage
- Ensures accessibility: tokens remain affordable for the poorest communities
- Per-capita allocation: ~14,925 WMP per person across the Americas

## Value Mechanisms
1. **Transaction fees**: 2-12% in WMP on all platform activity
2. **Forex demand**: WMP↔USD/BRL/MXN/ARS/COP/PEN/CLP conversions
3. **Staking**: Proof of Sovereignty validators earn WMP
4. **Burn**: 0.1% of each transaction burned (deflationary)
5. **Utility**: Required for premium services, governance voting weight
6. **Remittances**: Cross-border P2P transfers across the Americas (0% fee)
7. **NFC Offline**: Transactions without internet in rural/indigenous communities

## Price Stability
- BDET Bank maintains WMP/USD peg band ($0.0001-$0.001)
- Reserve fund: 10% supply (1T WMP) for market-making
- Algorithmic stabilization via AI Fortress
- No speculative trading incentives by design
- Backed by real economic activity across 35+ countries

## Anti-Speculation Measures

- Transfer cooldown: 1B+ Ⓦ requires 24h delay
- No listing on speculative exchanges without Council approval
- Priority: utility over speculation
