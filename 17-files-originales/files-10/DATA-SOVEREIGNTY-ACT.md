# Data Sovereignty Act
## Ierahkwa Sovereign Nation — Law 001

### Section 1: Definitions
- "Citizen Data" — any data created by, about, or belonging to a citizen
- "Platform Data" — aggregate, anonymized operational data
- "Sacred Data" — cultural, ceremonial, or traditional knowledge

### Section 2: Ownership
All Citizen Data is the exclusive property of the citizen who created it. The platform is a custodian, not an owner. Citizens may:
- Access all their data at any time
- Export all data in standard formats (JSON, CSV, PDF)
- Permanently delete all data immediately upon request
- Grant and revoke access to any entity

### Section 3: Prohibited Actions
The following are CRIMINAL offenses under Ierahkwa law:
- Selling citizen data to any entity
- Sharing citizen data without explicit consent
- Using citizen data for advertising targeting
- Training AI models on citizen data without consent
- Accessing encrypted citizen data through any backdoor
- Retaining citizen data after deletion request

### Section 4: Sacred Data Protection
Sacred, ceremonial, and traditional knowledge has the highest protection level:
- May only be stored with community Elder approval
- May never be shared outside the community
- May never be used for AI training
- Community may delete from all systems at any time
- Access requires cryptographic community key

### Section 5: Data Residency
All citizen data must reside on sovereign infrastructure:
- MameyNode blockchain for transactions and identity
- CloudSoberana for files and media
- No data stored on AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, or any foreign provider
- Exception: encrypted backups may be stored off-site if community approves

### Section 6: Breach Response
In the event of a data breach:
- Affected citizens notified within 4 hours
- Full transparency about scope and impact
- Free credit monitoring equivalent in Wampum
- Responsible parties face suspension from platform
- Post-mortem published publicly within 7 days

### Section 7: Enforcement
- Enforced by Guardian of Data (appointed by Consejo)
- Violations prosecuted by JusticiaSoberano platform
- Penalties: Suspension, Wampum fines, permanent ban
- All enforcement actions recorded on blockchain
