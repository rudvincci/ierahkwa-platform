# Constitution of the Consejo Digital Soberano
## Governing Body of Red Soberana Digital de las Americas

### Preamble
We, the indigenous peoples of Abya Yala, establish this digital sovereignty council to govern our shared technological infrastructure, ensuring it serves our communities, preserves our cultures, and protects our data for generations to come.

### Article I: Name and Purpose
The Consejo Digital Soberano ("Council") governs all operations of Red Soberana, including platforms, blockchain, banking, AI, and telecom infrastructure.

### Article II: Membership
- 1 seat per participating indigenous nation/community
- Maximum 574 seats (expandable by Council vote)
- Each member has equal voting power regardless of community size
- Observers: allied organizations (FILAC, COICA, NCAI) with voice but no vote

### Article III: Officers
- **Cacike Digital** — Chair, elected by Council for 4-year term
- **Guardian of Data** — Chief Privacy/Security officer
- **Keeper of Languages** — Oversees Atabey and cultural preservation
- **Treasurer of Wampum** — Oversees BDET Bank and economics
- **Council of Elders** — 7 members, advisory on cultural matters

### Article IV: Decision Making
- Routine matters: Simple majority
- Platform additions/removals: 2/3 majority
- Constitutional amendments: 3/4 majority
- Data policy changes: Unanimous consent required
- All votes recorded on MameyNode blockchain (immutable, transparent)

### Article V: Rights of Communities
1. Absolute ownership of their data
2. Right to fork any platform for local use
3. Right to add their language to Atabey
4. Right to propose and vote on platform changes
5. Right to withdraw from the network at any time with all data
6. Right to audit any system handling their data

### Article VI: Prohibitions
1. No selling of community data, ever
2. No advertising-based revenue
3. No partnership with entities that harm indigenous peoples
4. No surveillance of community members
5. No AI training on community data without explicit consent

### Article VII: Amendments
Proposed by any 3 members, debated for minimum 30 days on ForoSoberano, voted via VotoSoberano (blockchain), requires 3/4 majority.
