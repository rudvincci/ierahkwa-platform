# NEXUS Consejo — Technical Whitepaper

**Version**: 1.0.0 | **Date**: March 2026 | **Status**: Production

## Executive Summary

NEXUS Consejo is the sovereign digital government platform for indigenous nations, enabling self-governance through blockchain-verified voting, transparent policy management, and indigenous justice systems. It serves 19 nations and 574 tribal nations with culturally-appropriate governance tools.

## Problem Statement

Indigenous governance structures are forced to operate within colonial frameworks. Digital government tools designed for Western democracies ignore indigenous consensus-building, clan systems, and traditional governance. Sovereign governance technology must reflect indigenous values: collective decision-making, elder councils, and seven-generation thinking.

## Solution Architecture

### Governance Stack
1. **Quadratic Voting**: Weighted voting preventing wealth concentration
2. **Tribal Council Platform**: Multi-level governance (clan → tribe → nation)
3. **Treaty Management**: Blockchain-verified treaty records with all parties
4. **Sovereign Identity**: Self-sovereign identity (SSI) using DID standards
5. **Parliament**: Digital chamber for inter-nation legislative processes

### Consensus Mechanisms
- Traditional: Elder council approval workflows
- Modern: Quadratic voting with tribal weighting
- Hybrid: Proposal → Community discussion → Elder review → Vote → Blockchain record

### Justice System
- Restorative justice workflows (indigenous tradition)
- Case management with privacy protections
- Evidence chain on MameyNode blockchain
- Multi-lingual legal document support

## Security Model

- All governance records on immutable blockchain
- Zero-knowledge proofs for anonymous voting
- Multi-signature approval for policy changes
- End-to-end encrypted deliberations

## Roadmap

- Q1 2026: E-government portal + voting system (DONE)
- Q2 2026: Sovereign identity rollout across 5 pilot nations
- Q3 2026: Inter-nation treaty management system
- Q4 2026: Full digital governance for all 19 nations
