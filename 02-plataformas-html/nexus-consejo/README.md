# NEXUS Consejo — Government & Governance

> Sovereign digital government for indigenous self-determination

## Overview

NEXUS Consejo provides the digital government infrastructure for 19 sovereign nations and 574 tribal nations. It includes e-governance portals, voting systems (quadratic voting), parliament management, legal systems, justice portals, and democratic participation tools.

## Sub-Platforms (~27)

- E-Government Portal, Parliament Management, Tribal Council Platform
- Quadratic Voting System, Referendum Engine, Census System
- Legal Document Management, Justice Portal, Court System
- Citizen Identity (Sovereign ID), Document Verification
- Policy Management, Inter-Nation Diplomacy Platform
- Tax Administration, Land Registry, Treaty Management
