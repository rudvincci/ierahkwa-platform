# NEXUS Academia — University & Research

> Sovereign higher education and research infrastructure

## Overview

NEXUS Academia provides university management, research collaboration, academic publishing, PhD program management, and scholarly databases. Designed to support indigenous academic institutions and research centers.

## Sub-Platforms (~5)

- Universidad Soberana (University Management System)
- Research Collaboration Platform
- Academic Publishing & Journal System
- PhD/Graduate Program Management
- Indigenous Knowledge Research Database
