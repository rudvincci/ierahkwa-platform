# NEXUS Salud — Technical Whitepaper

**Version**: 1.0.0 | **Date**: March 2026 | **Status**: Production

## Executive Summary

NEXUS Salud delivers sovereign healthcare technology bridging modern medicine with traditional healing practices. Telemedicine reaches the most remote communities, AI diagnostics compensate for physician shortages, and all health data remains under indigenous control.

## Problem Statement

Indigenous communities face the worst health outcomes of any demographic: limited access to clinics, cultural insensitivity in healthcare delivery, health records stored in foreign systems, and zero integration with traditional medicine practices that have sustained communities for millennia.

## Solution Architecture

### Telemedicine
- Video consultations with real-time translation (14 languages)
- Offline medical form capture with sync-on-connect
- Remote diagnostic tools (dermatology AI, symptom checker)
- Integration with community health workers

### Health Records
- FHIR-compliant EHR with indigenous extensions
- Patient-controlled data with sovereign encryption
- Integration with pharmacy and lab systems
- Traditional medicine treatment records (elder-authorized)

### Health AI
- Symptom triage trained on indigenous health data
- Diagnostic imaging AI for remote communities
- Epidemic prediction using environmental data (NEXUS Tierra)
- Drug interaction checker with traditional remedies

## Security Model

- HIPAA-equivalent sovereign health data protection
- Patient consent required for all data access
- Health records encrypted with patient-held keys
- Anonymous epidemiological analytics (differential privacy)

## Roadmap

- Q1 2026: Telemedicine + EHR (DONE)
- Q2 2026: AI diagnostics for 5 common conditions
- Q3 2026: Traditional medicine database (elder-controlled)
- Q4 2026: Cross-nation health data sharing (with consent)
