# NEXUS Salud — Health & Wellness

> Sovereign healthcare infrastructure for indigenous communities

## Overview

NEXUS Salud provides telemedicine, electronic health records, pharmacy management, wellness platforms, and health AI for indigenous communities. Designed for remote healthcare delivery with offline capability and traditional medicine integration.

## Sub-Platforms (~9)

- Telemedicine Platform, Electronic Health Records (EHR)
- Pharmacy Management, Lab Results Portal
- Mental Health & Wellness, Community Health Dashboard
- Traditional Medicine Database, Emergency Health Alert
- Health AI Diagnostics
