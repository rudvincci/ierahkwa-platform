# NEXUS Voces — Technical Whitepaper

**Version**: 1.0.0 | **Date**: March 2026 | **Status**: Production

## Executive Summary

NEXUS Voces delivers sovereign social platforms where indigenous peoples communicate, organize, and share culture without corporate surveillance. E2E encrypted messaging, federated social networks, and indigenous language NLP ensure digital self-expression with full privacy.

## Problem Statement

Big Tech social platforms censor indigenous content, mine indigenous data for profit, lack indigenous language support, and have been used as tools of cultural erasure. Indigenous communities need self-governed communication spaces.

## Solution Architecture

### Federated Social Network
- ActivityPub protocol for inter-community federation
- Matrix protocol for encrypted messaging
- Jitsi-compatible video conferencing (sovereign hosted)
- Indigenous content moderation by tribal councils

### Privacy Architecture
- E2E encryption (Signal protocol) for all messaging
- Zero metadata collection
- Onion routing for anonymous communication
- Self-destructing messages for sensitive discussions

## Roadmap

- Q1 2026: Core social + messaging (DONE)
- Q2 2026: Video conferencing + live streaming
- Q3 2026: Indigenous language NLP and auto-translation
- Q4 2026: Federated network across 19 nations
