# NEXUS Voces — Social Media & Communications

> Sovereign social platforms for indigenous voices and community building

## Overview

NEXUS Voces provides sovereign social media, messaging, video conferencing, forums, and community platforms. Zero surveillance, zero data mining, full indigenous language support. Replaces Facebook, WhatsApp, Twitter, and Zoom.

## Sub-Platforms (~16)

- Social Network, Messaging, Video Conferencing
- Forums, Community Platform, Live Streaming
- Podcast Platform, News Aggregator, Blog Engine
- Photo Sharing, Short Video, Event Management
