# NEXUS Tesoro — Technical Whitepaper

**Version**: 1.0.0 | **Date**: March 2026 | **Status**: Production

## Executive Summary

NEXUS Tesoro provides complete financial sovereignty for indigenous nations through blockchain-based banking, the IGT token economy (10T WAMPUM supply), DeFi protocols, and sovereign payment infrastructure. It enables economic self-determination free from traditional banking gatekeepers.

## Problem Statement

Indigenous peoples are systematically excluded from or exploited by traditional financial systems. Banks refuse service to remote communities, currency exchange disadvantages indigenous economies, and financial surveillance threatens indigenous autonomy.

## Solution Architecture

### MameyNode Blockchain
- Custom sovereign blockchain with Proof-of-Stake consensus
- 103 IGT token types for different economic functions
- Genesis Validator with tribal governance weighting
- Quadratic voting for economic policy decisions

### Token Economy
- Total Supply: 10 Trillion WAMPUM
- Governance tokens, utility tokens, stablecoins
- Cross-border indigenous trade settlement
- Micro-lending and community investment pools

### Banking Infrastructure
- Sovereign banking with zero foreign dependencies
- Real-time forex trading with indigenous pairs
- Insurance products designed for indigenous needs
- Crowdfunding for community development projects

## Security Model

- Multi-signature wallets with tribal council approval
- Post-quantum encryption for all transactions
- Smart contract auditing and formal verification
- Anti-money-laundering with indigenous privacy protections
