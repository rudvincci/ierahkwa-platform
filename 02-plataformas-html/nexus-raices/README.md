# NEXUS Raices — Culture & Heritage

> Preserving indigenous languages, art, music, and traditions through sovereign technology

## Overview

NEXUS Raices is the cultural heart of the Ierahkwa digital nation. It provides platforms for indigenous language preservation, art galleries, music production, oral history archives, and cultural education. Ensures indigenous heritage survives and thrives in the digital age.

## Sub-Platforms (~16)

- Indigenous Language Learning Platform (14 languages)
- Digital Art Gallery, Music Production Studio
- Oral History Archive, Cultural Encyclopedia
- Traditional Medicine Knowledge Base (privacy-protected)
- Ceremony Calendar, Artisan Marketplace
- Dance/Music Recording Studio, Story Preservation
