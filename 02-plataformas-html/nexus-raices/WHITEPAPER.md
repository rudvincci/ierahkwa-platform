# NEXUS Raices — Technical Whitepaper

**Version**: 1.0.0 | **Date**: March 2026 | **Status**: Production

## Executive Summary

NEXUS Raices leverages sovereign technology to preserve and revitalize indigenous cultures facing extinction. With 14 indigenous language NLP models, digital art platforms, and oral history archives, it ensures cultural continuity for future generations while respecting sacred knowledge protocols.

## Problem Statement

Indigenous cultures face unprecedented threats: languages are dying at the rate of one every two weeks, cultural artifacts are held in foreign museums, traditional knowledge is appropriated without consent, and digital platforms lack indigenous representation. Technology must serve as a shield for cultural preservation.

## Solution Architecture

### Language Preservation
- NLP models for 14 indigenous languages (Mohawk, Cherokee, Navajo, Quechua, etc.)
- Speech-to-text and text-to-speech for oral languages
- Interactive language learning with gamification
- Indigenous script rendering and keyboard support

### Cultural Archive
- IPFS-based distributed storage for cultural artifacts
- Blockchain provenance for digital art and artifacts
- Tiered access: public knowledge → community knowledge → sacred knowledge
- Elder-controlled access permissions for sensitive content

## Security Model

- Sacred knowledge requires elder council approval for access
- Cultural artifacts tracked on blockchain for provenance
- Anti-appropriation protections (usage tracking, license enforcement)
- Community consent for all knowledge digitization

## Roadmap

- Q1 2026: Language learning + art gallery (DONE)
- Q2 2026: Oral history archive with AI transcription
- Q3 2026: Traditional medicine database (elder-controlled)
- Q4 2026: Pan-indigenous cultural exchange platform
