## Platform-Specific Best Practices

### LinkedIn

**Best For**: Professional audience, B2B content, thought leadership

**Best Practices**:
- Long-form posts (1,300-2,000 characters)
- Professional tone
- Industry insights
- Thought leadership content

**Posting Times**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM

---

### Twitter/X

**Best For**: Quick updates, news, engagement

**Best Practices**:
- Concise posts (280 characters)
- Use threads for longer content
- Engage with replies
- Use relevant hashtags

**Posting Times**: Monday-Friday, 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM

---

### Facebook

**Best For**: Broader audience, community building

**Best Practices**:
- Longer posts (1,000+ characters)
- Visual content
- Community engagement
- Friendly tone

**Posting Times**: Monday-Friday, 9 AM-3 PM

---

## Content Creation Guidelines

### Content Types

**Educational**: How-to guides, tutorials, tips  
**News**: Announcements, updates, industry news  
**Engagement**: Questions, polls, discussions  
**Visual**: Images, infographics, videos

## Engagement Strategies

### Engagement Tactics

- Share user-generated content
- Participate in relevant conversations
- Use polls and surveys
