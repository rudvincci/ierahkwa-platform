---

## Response Protocols

### Response Timeline

**Immediate** (0-1 hour):
- Acknowledge issue
- Initial statement

**Short-term** (1-24 hours):
- Investigation
- Detailed response
- Media outreach

**Long-term** (24+ hours):
- Resolution
- Follow-up
- Lessons learned

## Social Media Crisis Response

### Response Guidelines

**Immediate Response**:
- Acknowledge on all platforms
- Direct to official statement
- Monitor mentions

**Follow-up**:
- Regular updates
- Address concerns
- Provide resolution

## Best Practices

### Crisis Management Guidelines

- **Speed**: Respond quickly
- **Accuracy**: Verify facts before responding
- **Transparency**: Be honest and transparent
- **Consistency**: Maintain consistent messaging
- **Follow-up**: Provide regular updates
