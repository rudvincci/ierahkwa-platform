## Press Release Best Practices

### Headline Guidelines

- **Length**: 8-12 words
- **Style**: Active voice, compelling
- **Content**: Who, what, why
- **Tone**: Professional, newsworthy

### Opening Paragraph

- **5 W's**: Who, What, When, Where, Why
- **Hook**: Compelling opening
- **Key Facts**: Most important information first

### Body Paragraphs

- **Details**: Expand on opening
- **Quotes**: Include relevant quotes
- **Statistics**: Use data to support claims
- **Structure**: One idea per paragraph

### Closing

- **Call to Action**: What should readers do?
- **Contact**: How to get more information
- **About**: Company description
