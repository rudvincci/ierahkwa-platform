## News Article Format

### Standard News Article Structure

**Headline**: Compelling, newsworthy, 8-12 words

**Lead Paragraph**: Who, What, When, Where, Why (5 W's)

**Body Paragraphs**: 
- Supporting details
- Quotes
- Statistics
- Background information

**Conclusion**: 
- Summary
- Call to action
- Contact information

## Best Practices

### Writing Guidelines

- **Accuracy**: Verify all facts and statistics
- **Clarity**: Use clear, concise language
- **Balance**: Present multiple perspectives
- **Attribution**: Always attribute quotes and statistics
- **Fact-Check**: Verify with press@mamey.io before publication
