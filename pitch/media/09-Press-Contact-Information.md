## Interview Requests

### How to Request an Interview

Please contact press@mamey.io with the following information:

1. **Topic/Angle**: What is the focus of your story?
2. **Deadline**: When do you need the interview?
3. **Format**: Print, broadcast, podcast, online, etc.
4. **Preferred Date/Time**: When would you like to schedule?
5. **Questions**: Any specific questions or topics?

**Response Time**: We aim to respond within 24 hours
