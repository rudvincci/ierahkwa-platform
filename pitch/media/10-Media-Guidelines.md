## Brand Guidelines

### Company Name

**Correct Usage**:
- Mamey Technologies
- Mamey (when context is clear)

**Incorrect Usage**:
- Mamey Tech
- Mamey Tech Inc.
- Mamey Technologies Inc.

### Tagline

**Official Tagline**: "Building better financial infrastructure for the sovereign era"

**Usage**: Use when appropriate, especially in first reference

## Logo Usage

### Logo Guidelines

**Approved Logo Files**: Available upon request from press@mamey.io

**Usage Rules**:
- Use approved logo files only
- Maintain minimum clear space (equal to height of "M" in logo)
- Do not alter colors or proportions
- Do not place on busy backgrounds
- Maintain aspect ratio

**Logo Variations**:
- Full color (preferred)
- Black (for light backgrounds)
- White (for dark backgrounds)

## Terminology Guidelines

### Preferred Terms

**Technology**:
- "Complete sovereign financial infrastructure ecosystem"
- "Production-ready blockchain platform"
- "Block Lattice architecture"
- "DPoS consensus"

**Performance**:
- "24,356+ TPS" (not "24,356 TPS" - the "+" indicates measured minimum)
- "10.3x faster than Visa"
- "< 50ms latency" (not "under 50ms")
- "5.9ms average finality"

**Platforms**:
- "MameyNode Blockchain" (not "MameyNode" alone in first reference)
- "Banking Libraries & Microservices"
- "Government Services"
- "Holistic Medicine" (not "Healthcare Platform" - use full name)

### Terms to Avoid

- "Cryptocurrency" (we're a blockchain infrastructure platform, not a cryptocurrency)
- "Crypto" (use "blockchain" or "blockchain technology")
- "DeFi" (we're focused on regulated financial infrastructure)
- "Startup" (we're an established technology company)

## Attribution Requirements

### Required Attribution

**Quotes**: Always attribute quotes to the speaker

**Statistics**: Attribute statistics to Mamey Technologies or source document

**Images**: Credit Mamey Technologies for all images

**Example**:
"According to Mamey Technologies, the platform achieves 24,356+ transactions per second."

## Interview Guidelines

### Interview Process

1. **Request**: Contact press@mamey.io with interview request
2. **Coordination**: We'll coordinate scheduling and logistics
3. **Preparation**: We'll provide background materials
4. **Conduct**: Interview with spokesperson
5. **Follow-up**: We'll provide any additional information needed

### Interview Best Practices

- **Be Prepared**: Review background materials
- **Be Specific**: Ask specific questions
- **Be Respectful**: Respect time constraints
- **Be Accurate**: Verify facts and statistics
