## Best Practices

### Pitching Guidelines

- **Personalize**: Research journalist and outlet
- **Be Concise**: Keep pitches brief and focused
- **Be Relevant**: Match story to journalist's beat
- **Follow Up**: Follow up appropriately (not too soon, not too late)
- **Provide Value**: Offer exclusive angles or insights
