01 FOUNDING VISION

The Vision — Gayanashagowa

A digital nation founded on the Great Law of Peace

A sovereign nation where 72 million indigenous people across 19 countries exercise full digital sovereignty over their identity, governance, education, healthcare, and economy — governed by the principles of the Gayanashagowa, the Great Law of Peace.

Zero Taxes

Citizens pay no taxes. Revenue is generated through sustainable platform fees on commercial services, not from the people.

Free Education

Universal access to K-12 and university education in 43 languages, including 37 indigenous languages. AI-powered curriculum.

Free Healthcare

Telemedicine, prescription management, and integration with indigenous healing practices — universally accessible at no cost.

Self-Governed Technology

All infrastructure is owned and operated by the nation. No external dependencies. Full digital sovereignty over data and systems.

04 DEMOCRATIC GOVERNANCE

Blockchain-based voting and decentralized decision-making

Every citizen's voice matters. Voto Soberano is a blockchain-based voting system running on MameyNode, ensuring that every vote is transparent, immutable, and verifiable. GovernanceDAO enables decentralized decision-making at every level.

Voto Soberano

  • Blockchain-based voting on MameyNode
