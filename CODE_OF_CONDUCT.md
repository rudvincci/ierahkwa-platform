# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, the contributors and maintainers of the Ierahkwa Sovereign Platform, pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, with special commitment to honoring indigenous knowledge, traditions, and sovereignty.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior

- Respect for indigenous cultural knowledge and traditions
- Language inclusivity — supporting 37 indigenous languages and 6 global languages
- Community-first approach to development decisions
- Constructive collaboration across cultures and technical backgrounds
- Acknowledgment of indigenous intellectual property rights

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Cultural appropriation or misrepresentation of indigenous traditions
- Dismissal of indigenous perspectives or knowledge systems
- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary language
- Colonial attitudes in code practices or project governance
- Unauthorized commercialization of cultural content

## Enforcement

Community leaders will take appropriate action in response to violations:

1. **Correction** — Private guidance on the impact of the behavior
2. **Warning** — Formal warning with consequences for continued behavior
3. **Temporary Ban** — Temporary removal from community spaces
4. **Permanent Ban** — Permanent removal from the project
